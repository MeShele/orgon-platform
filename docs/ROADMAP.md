## Regulatory Compliance (Kyrgyz Republic)
- **Law:** KR "On Virtual Assets" №12 (21.01.2022)
- **Decree:** Government Decree №625 (30.09.2025)
- **Regulator:** Finnadzor (Financial Supervision Service)

ORGON helps licensed virtual asset providers comply with:
- Separate wallet storage per client
- AML/KYC/KYT monitoring
- Transaction risk scoring
- Mandatory reporting to Finnadzor
